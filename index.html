Data Visualisation for Health and Care

The community was launched in Febuary 2022, and building off the back of our first event as part of Analyst X we formed a committe of folk working in data visualisation across CSUs, Trusts, NHS Digital, NHS England and others.

We aim to

  • Become the go-to community for people working in data visualisation in health and care Provide a framework for knowledge sharing.
  • Support people working across a range of tools, technologies and platforms.
  • Make learning and development material available and accessible to upskill and to professionalise our workforce.
  • Provide an innovative, colloborative and safe space for users to generate ideas
